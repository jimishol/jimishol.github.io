computeDaycycle (dayTime)
Computes sky factor and environment brightness from an hour value.

Parameters:

  • dayTime number 
      Current time in hours (0–24)

Returns:

  1. number sunFactor Normalized sun factor (0–1) for skyExt:setDaytime()
  2. number envBright Calculated environment brightness (0–constants.maxBright) for dream:setSky()
