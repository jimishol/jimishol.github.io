Module src.utils.key_estimation

Key Estimation Module for Umbilic-Surface Harmony.

Detects the Diatonic Key based on the geometric activation of Dominant and Subdominant surfaces. Uses src.materials as the single source of truth for surface activation physics.

WARNING: TOPOLOGICAL BIAS (WESTERN TONALITY) This estimator applies a “Common Practice” filter to the neutral 3D lattice. It assumes a Key is formed by a cluster of 4 adjacent Surfaces. Non-Western or experimental geometries (e.g., Symmetric/Modal) may require their own dedicated estimation modules.
