Fields

key_stability_threshold
Stability Threshold (Debounce) for Key Detection. The number of consecutive frames a key candidate must be detected before updating the display.

Usage:

    Low (3-5): Responsive, catches fast jazz changes, risks glissando flicker.
High (10-15): Very stable, "Classical" feel, ignores grace notes.
Recommended: 6 (~100ms at 60fps).
